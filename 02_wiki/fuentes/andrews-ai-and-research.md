# Andrews — AI and Research (2026)

## Referencia

Isaiah Andrews. `Some Thoughts on AI and Research`. Public note, 2026.

## Resumen

Andrews argues that if models become much better at execution while remaining weaker at judgement, taste, and problem selection, the value of human criterion rises instead of falling.

## Takeaways para Ateneo-KB

- Verification is not optional; it becomes a core skill.
- Better execution by models shifts the bottleneck toward judgement.
- Research infrastructure should treat auditing as a first-class operation.

## Relación con Ateneo

This source helps bridge the technical pattern toward a humanities-facing claim: the next problem is no longer access alone, but criterion.
