# Azaustre — Segundo cerebro Karpathy (2026)

## Referencia

Carlos Azaustre. `Mi segundo cerebro: cómo construí una wiki que la IA mantiene sola (al estilo Karpathy)`. Public article, 5 April 2026.

## Resumen

Azaustre operationalizes the Karpathy pattern with real automation: ingestion from external feeds, lint, health checks, and a loop where outputs update the wiki again.

## Takeaways para Ateneo-KB

- The pattern can become an operating system, not just a note-taking idea.
- Output feedback loops are valuable and should be formalized.
- Automation alone does not solve the problem of epistemic discipline.

## Relación con Ateneo

Azaustre validates the architecture but not the humanistic layer. The public difference of Ateneo-KB is not that it can automate, but that it can separate what is supported from what is interpretive.
