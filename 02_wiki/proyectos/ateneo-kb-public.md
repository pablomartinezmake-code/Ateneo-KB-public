# Ateneo-KB Public

## Descripción

Public, sanitized edition of an editorial knowledge base for humanities-oriented AI research.

## Por qué existe

To show that the interesting difference is not another AI notebook, but a method that keeps provenance visible and separates evidence from interpretation.

## Evidencia firme

- The repository exposes raw captures, curated notes, concepts, outputs, templates, and scripts.
- It generates a graph, a lineage map, and an editorial dashboard from the same sample corpus.
- The public sample is intentionally small so the method remains legible.

## Lectura abierta

This public edition is not a clone of a private vault. It is closer to a method release or a reproducible editorial sample.

## Hipótesis abierta

- A reusable template may help establish a public category for humanities-facing `LLM wiki` systems with editorial governance.
