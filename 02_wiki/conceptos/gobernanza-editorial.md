# Gobernanza editorial

## Definición

An explicit layer of human review that decides what enters the curated wiki, at what confidence level, and under what distinction between evidence and interpretation.

## Evidencia firme

- The Karpathy pattern is structurally reusable across domains.
- Azaustre shows that the pattern can be automated and scaled operationally.
- Neither pattern, by itself, forces a visible distinction between stronger support and open reading.

## Lectura abierta

Governance is what turns a knowledge base into an editorial instrument. It is the difference between a memory system that accumulates notes and a research system that can defend why one statement is firmer than another.

## Hipótesis abierta

- In humanities-facing systems, governance may become a more public differentiator than model size or ingestion volume.
