# Why this is not another second brain

## Summary

This sample repository is public on purpose, but minimal on purpose as well. Its job is not to prove that AI can collect notes. Its job is to show a stricter editorial pattern.

## Evidence

- Raw captures remain immutable.
- Curated notes declare their sources and confidence.
- Concept notes separate stronger support from open interpretation.
- Outputs derive from curated material instead of bypassing it.

## Reading

The public opportunity here is not merely `knowledge management`. It is a humanities-facing claim about governance: when AI becomes good at compilation, the visible difference shifts toward judgement, provenance, and restraint.

## Open question

- Can this pattern become a reusable template for scholars who need citability and interpretation to coexist without collapsing into one another?
