# What Stoic assent has to do with LLM knowledge bases

## Summary

This note exists as a portability proof. The public sample should not depend only on copyrighted or specialist corpora. A short Stoic chain shows that the same editorial method can travel into a fully auditable public-domain example.

## Evidence

- Epictetus distinguishes between what appears and the judgement we add to what appears.
- Ateneo-KB distinguishes between retrieved material and endorsed claim.
- The Stoic sample can be audited end to end because the raw passage is public domain and included in the repository.

## Reading

In that sense, disciplined assent becomes a useful metaphor for editorial governance. A model may surface passages quickly, but a knowledge base should not assent to them as evidence until provenance, confidence, and review have been made explicit.
